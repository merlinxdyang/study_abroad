# Recommendation Letter Template

Use this only as a recommender-facing scaffold. The recommender must write, revise, and approve the final letter in their own voice.

To the Admissions Committee,

I am writing in support of [applicant_name]'s application to [program_name].

I know the applicant through [course/research/project/advising context]. In this setting, the applicant demonstrated [specific ability 1] and [specific ability 2].

One example is [specific episode]. The applicant [action], which resulted in [result or observed growth]. This evidence is relevant to [program_name] because [verified program requirement or fit point].

Based on this experience, I believe the applicant is prepared to contribute to and benefit from the program. I recommend them for admission.

Sincerely,

[recommender_name]
[title]
[institution]
[contact]
