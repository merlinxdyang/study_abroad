# Cover Letter Template

Use this as a drafting scaffold. Replace every bracketed item with verified applicant or program evidence.

Dear Admissions Committee,

I am writing to apply for [program_name] at [school_name]. My strongest fit for this program is [one applicant strength tied to one verified program feature].

My academic background has prepared me for the program through [relevant courses], [GPA or academic distinction if useful], and [research/project experience]. In [specific project/course/research], I [action + result], which connects directly to [program requirement or theme].

I am particularly interested in [course/lab/faculty/research direction/program feature] because [specific reason based on applicant goals or prior work]. This fit would allow me to [concrete learning or research outcome].

After the program, I plan to [short-term goal]. In the longer term, I hope to [long-term goal], and the program's [verified feature] is important to that trajectory because [reason].

Thank you for considering my application. I would welcome the opportunity to contribute [specific contribution] to [program/community].

Sincerely,

[applicant_name]
